About Company

Leading the Future of Vertical Transportation

PT FUJIZY LIFT CONSTRUCTION INDONESIA is a comprehensive modern elevator manufacturing enterprise integrating design, production, sales, installation, and maintenance services. With over 10,000 square meters of production facilities and state-of-the-art equipment, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project.

Our extensive product range includes passenger elevators, residential elevators, observation elevators, hospital elevators, villa elevators, freight elevators, car elevators, hydraulic elevators, stretcher elevators, escalators, and moving walkways. We also specialize in custom non-standard elevator solutions tailored to your specific requirements.
